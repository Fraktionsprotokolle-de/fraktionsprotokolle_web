# <p style="text-align: center;">The Team behind fraktionsprotokolle.de</p>

---

The **[Commission for the History of Parliamentarism and Political Parties](http://www.kgparl.de/) (KGParl)** is responsible for the conception and implementation of the website, which has presented the edition project *“Parliamentary Party Groups in the German Bundestag”* since 2017.
