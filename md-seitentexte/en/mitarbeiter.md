# <p style="text-align: center;">Working Group Behind fraktionsprotokolle.de</p>

------

The **[Commission for the History of Parliamentarism and Political Parties](http://www.kgparl.de/) (KGParl)** is responsible for the conception and implementation of the website dedicated to the edition program *»Parliamentary Groups in the German Bundestag«, which has been online since 2017.
