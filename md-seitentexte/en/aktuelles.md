# <p style="text-align: center;">Current Information on the Edition »Fraktionsprotokolle.de«</p>



Since **January 2022**, the new TEI-XML edition has been available online. The technical infrastructure of the website is largely based on the software [tei-Publisher](https://teipublisher.com/index.html). The documents themselves are encoded in TEI-XML. Further details can be found in the editorial guidelines.

The edition includes all documents published to date within the project.

**As of February 2026**, the following session minutes have been published in TEI-XML:

- CDU/CSU Parliamentary Group: 1949–1976
- SPD Parliamentary Group: 1949–1980
- FDP Parliamentary Group: 1949–1983
- CSU Regional Group: 1949–1983
- Parliamentary Group DIE GRÜNEN: 1983–1990

## Publication of the Introductions to the Printed Edition Volumes

All scholarly introductions that have so far appeared only in print will gradually be made available on the edition’s website. We begin with the publication of the introduction by [Volker Stalmann](https://kgparl.de/personen/volker-stalmann/) to the volume: [»Die CSU-Landesgruppe im Deutschen Bundestag. Sitzungsprotokolle 1972–1983«](https://kgparl.de/publikationen/die-csu-landesgruppe-im-deutschen-bundestag-sitzungsprotokolle-1972-1983/).
