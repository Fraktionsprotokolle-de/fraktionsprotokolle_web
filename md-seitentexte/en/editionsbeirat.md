# <p style="text-align: center;">Advisory Board for the Edition<p/>

------



Through the provision of additional funding by the German Bundestag, the edition and research focus »Parliamentary Groups in the German Bundestag« was restructured in spring 2013, ensuring the continuation of the Commission for the History of Parliamentarism and Political Parties (KGParl)’s established editorial work.

o provide scholarly guidance and advisory support for the long-term edition program, an Advisory Board for the Edition was established. In addition to responsible representatives of the KGParl, the board includes the directors of the participating archives of the party-affiliated foundations, as well as representatives of the parliamentary groups and parliamentary groups (Fraktionen and Gruppen) of the German Bundestag whose records are being edited within the project.

The responsibilities of the Advisory Board include reviewing the progress of the work, clarifying financial and organizational matters, and maintaining communicative liaison with the political sphere.
